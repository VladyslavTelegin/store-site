What connects our apps

Built on shared principles

Every iOS Craft Studio app follows the same philosophy. Four values that shape every decision, every feature, every line of code.

🔒
Privacy is architecture
Not a toggle. Not a setting. Privacy is baked into the foundation. Zero servers, zero accounts, zero cloud storage. Your data physically cannot leave your device.
🧠
Explainable logic
Every recommendation comes with a "why." Deterministic algorithms, not black-box AI. You can always trace back the reasoning behind any suggestion.
❤️
No guilt, no shame
Missed goals are data points, not failures. No streak anxiety, no red indicators, no passive-aggressive notifications. Calm confidence over gamification.
⚡
Premium craft
100% native SwiftUI. Silky 120fps animations. Pixel-perfect design. No web wrappers, no cross-platform shortcuts. Built for Apple hardware.

Privacy Promise

Your device. Your data.

We do not just avoid collecting your data. We architected our apps so it is physically impossible for us to access it.

🛡️
On-device only
Health data
Preferences

Everything stays here
All processing, storage, and intelligence happens on your iPhone or Apple Watch. Period.
🔒
No accounts required
Open the app and start using it. No email, no phone number, no sign-up flow. Ever.
🚫
No analytics or tracking
We do not use Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or any third-party analytics SDK. Zero telemetry.

We do not operate servers. There is no backend, no API, no database with your information in it.

Your data is never sold, shared, or transmitted. Not to advertisers, not to partners, not to anyone.
