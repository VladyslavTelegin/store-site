What it does

Each step has a purpose.

The app guides you through clinically validated techniques, one at a time, so you don't have to remember anything.

Step-by-step guide

Follow along without having to think

Safety anchor, breathing, grounding, reassurance. Each step appears only when you're ready for it.

Box breathing (4-4-4)

Slow your heart rate with guided breathing

Inhale 4 seconds, hold 4 seconds, exhale 4 seconds. The app counts for you with haptic feedback.

5-4-3-2-1 grounding

Come back to the present moment

5 things you see, 4 you hear, 3 you touch, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. Step by step until you're here again.

Common questions

Honest answers.

Can an app really help during a panic attack?

Yes. Guided breathing and grounding are clinically validated techniques. The app just makes them accessible when you can't think straight.

What if I can't even look at my phone?

That's why we built Apple Watch support. A tap on your wrist starts the guide. Haptic breathing cues work even with your eyes closed.

Is this a replacement for therapy?

No. This is a tool for the moment. If you experience frequent panic attacks, please seek professional help. nopaniq is there for when you need immediate support.
