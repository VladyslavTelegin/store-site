1. Our Privacy Philosophy

nopaniq stores everything on your device. We operate zero servers. We have zero access to your data. When you use nopaniq during a panic attack, nobody else knows — not us, not anyone.

Mental health data is among the most sensitive data there is. We chose a zero-server architecture specifically so this data can never be compromised, leaked, or shared.

2. Data We Access

nopaniq stores the following data locally on your device:

Data TypePurposeLeaves Device?
Session HistoryRecord of completed relief sessions (Pro)No
Personal AnchorsCustom safety statements you create (Pro)No
Haptic PreferenceVibration feedback toggleNo
SettingsApp preferencesNo

All data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

nopaniq does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email, or personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • HealthKit or health data of any kind
  • Any data from other apps
  • When or how often you experience panic attacks

We have no analytics SDK, no tracking pixels, no server logs, and no user database. We literally cannot identify you — and we designed it that way on purpose.

4. How Data Is Processed

All processing happens locally on your device. The guided relief flow runs entirely offline. Breathing timers and grounding exercises use no external services. nopaniq works fully offline — including on Apple Watch.

5. Data Storage

  • Encrypted at rest by iOS Data Protection
  • Sandboxed within the nopaniq app container
  • Included in iCloud backups only if you have iCloud backup enabled
  • Deleted entirely when you uninstall nopaniq

6. Third Parties

nopaniq does not share data with any third parties. No analytics, no ads, no crash reporting. The only external communication is Apple's StoreKit 2 for subscription verification.

7. Subscriptions & Payments

Optional Pro subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple. We have no access to your payment information or Apple ID.

8. Children's Privacy

nopaniq is not directed at children under 13. Since we collect no personal information, this concern is inherently mitigated.

9. Your Rights

  • Access: All data is on your device — you already have complete access.
  • Deletion: Uninstall nopaniq to delete all app data.
  • Restriction: Revoke notification permissions in Settings at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

Our core commitment — your data stays on your device — will not change. This is especially important for a mental health tool.
