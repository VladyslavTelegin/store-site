Privacy Policy

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

Somniq processes everything on your device. We operate zero servers. We have zero access to your data. Privacy is not a feature we added — it is the architecture we chose.

We believe your sleep data is among the most intimate data about you. It reveals your health, your habits, and your daily rhythms. That is why we built Somniq with a strict on-device-only architecture. There is no backend. There are no analytics endpoints. There is no way for us to see, access, or retrieve your data — because it simply never leaves your device.

2. Data We Access

Somniq accesses the following data categories exclusively through Apple HealthKit, and only with your explicit permission:

Data Type Purpose Leaves Device?
Sleep Analysis Determine sleep stages, duration, efficiency No
Heart Rate Physiological recovery analysis during sleep No
Resting Heart Rate Baseline comparison and readiness scoring No
Heart Rate Variability (HRV) Autonomic nervous system and recovery assessment No
Respiratory Rate Sleep quality supplementary metric No
Blood Oxygen Saturation Sleep quality supplementary metric No
Wrist Temperature Circadian rhythm and recovery indicator No
Workouts Analyze exercise timing impact on sleep No

Additionally, Somniq stores data you voluntarily enter within the app:

  • Daily check-ins — mood and energy ratings (1–5 scale), optional notes
  • Behavior logs — caffeine, alcohol, exercise, screen time, and other habits you choose to track
  • User preferences — notification settings, display preferences, sleep schedule goals

All of this data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. None of it is transmitted anywhere.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

To be absolutely clear, Somniq does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprints
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral telemetry
  • Crash reports (we do not use any crash reporting SDK)
  • Any data from other apps on your device
  • Your HealthKit data (we access it locally but never transmit it)

We have no analytics SDK, no tracking pixels, no server logs, and no user database. We literally cannot identify you.

4. Apple HealthKit

Somniq integrates with Apple HealthKit under Apple's strict HealthKit guidelines:

  • Read-only access. Somniq never writes data to HealthKit.
  • Explicit permission. You grant or deny access to each data type individually through the standard iOS Health permissions dialog.
  • Revocable at any time. You can revoke Somniq's HealthKit access in Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > Somniq.
  • Not shared. In compliance with Apple's HealthKit rules, HealthKit data is never shared with third parties, used for advertising, or sold.
  • Not transferred. HealthKit data accessed by Somniq is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted over any network.

5. How Data Is Processed

All data processing in Somniq happens locally on your iPhone or Apple Watch. The processing includes:

Statistical Analysis

Somniq calculates rolling baselines (14-day medians), performs outlier detection, and computes effect sizes (Cohen's d) to discover behavior-sleep correlations. These are standard statistical methods executed by your device's processor.

Readiness Scoring

A weighted algorithm combines sleep duration, efficiency, consistency, physiological metrics, and trends to produce a daily readiness score. The weights and calculations are fully deterministic and run on-device.

Recommendation Generation

Somniq generates personalized action plans and recovery windows based on your baseline data and recent patterns. No external AI service or cloud model is used. All logic is embedded in the app binary.

There are no network calls for data processing. Somniq works fully offline after installation.

6. Data Storage

All app data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework in a local database on your device. This data is:

  • Encrypted at rest by iOS Data Protection (tied to your device passcode)
  • Sandboxed within the Somniq app container — inaccessible to other apps
  • Included in iCloud backups only if you have iCloud backup enabled on your device (this is managed by iOS, not by Somniq)
  • Deleted entirely when you uninstall Somniq from your device

We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud storage. There is no "account" to delete because no account exists.

7. Third Parties

Somniq does not share data with any third parties. Specifically:

  • No analytics providers (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar)
  • No advertising networks (no ads of any kind)
  • No crash reporting services (no Firebase Crashlytics, Sentry, or similar)
  • No social integrations (no Facebook SDK, no social login)
  • No data brokers

The only external service Somniq communicates with is the Apple App Store for subscription verification, which is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit 2 framework and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

8. Subscriptions & Payments

Somniq offers optional Pro subscriptions through the Apple App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card number, or Apple ID.

Subscription details:

  • Payments are processed by Apple through your App Store account
  • Subscription status is verified on-device using Apple's StoreKit 2
  • No purchase data is transmitted to our servers (we have no servers)
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions

9. Children's Privacy

Somniq is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since Somniq collects no personal information from any user, this concern is inherently mitigated by our architecture.

10. Your Rights

Because Somniq operates with a zero-server, on-device-only architecture:

  • Access: All your data is on your device. You already have complete access.
  • Deletion: Uninstall Somniq to delete all app data. Alternatively, revoke HealthKit permissions to stop data access.
  • Portability: Your health data remains in Apple Health and is portable through Apple's ecosystem.
  • Restriction: You can restrict specific data types through Health > Data Access.
  • Objection: You can stop all processing by simply closing or uninstalling the app.

For users in the European Union, California, or other jurisdictions with specific privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.): Somniq's architecture means we are not a data controller or processor of your personal data in any networked sense. All data remains under your control on your device.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date above. Since Somniq does not collect email addresses, we cannot notify you via email. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Our core commitment — that your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party — will not change. If we ever change our architecture in a way that affects this commitment, we will require your explicit consent through an in-app prompt before any data leaves your device.
