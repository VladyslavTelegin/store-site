somniq uses a zero-server architecture. Your sleep data never leaves your device. This policy explains exactly what we access and what we don't.

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

somniq stores everything on your device. We operate zero servers. We have zero access to your data. Privacy is not a feature — it is the architecture.

Your sleep patterns are deeply personal. How you slept, what affected your rest, how you felt in the morning — that data belongs to you and only you.

2. Data We Access

somniq stores the following data locally on your device:

Data TypePurposeLeaves Device?
Sleep Quality RatingsDaily morning check-in scoresNo
Sleep DurationHow long you sleptNo
Factor TagsWhat might have affected sleepNo
Trend DataPattern detection over time (Pro)No
Theme PreferenceUI customizationNo

All data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

somniq does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email, or personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • HealthKit or health data of any kind
  • Any data from other apps

We have no analytics SDK, no tracking pixels, no server logs, and no user database. We literally cannot identify you.

4. How Data Is Processed

All processing happens locally on your device. Pattern detection and correlation analysis run entirely on-device. There are no network calls for data processing. somniq works fully offline after installation.

5. Data Storage

  • Encrypted at rest by iOS Data Protection
  • Sandboxed within the somniq app container
  • Included in iCloud backups only if you have iCloud backup enabled
  • Deleted entirely when you uninstall somniq

6. Third Parties

somniq does not share data with any third parties. No analytics, no ads, no crash reporting. The only external communication is Apple's StoreKit 2 for subscription verification.

7. Subscriptions & Payments

Optional Pro subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple. We have no access to your payment information or Apple ID.

8. Children's Privacy

somniq is not directed at children under 13. Since we collect no personal information, this concern is inherently mitigated.

9. Your Rights

  • Access: All data is on your device — you already have complete access.
  • Deletion: Uninstall somniq to delete all app data.
  • Restriction: Revoke notification permissions in Settings at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

Our core commitment — your data stays on your device — will not change.
