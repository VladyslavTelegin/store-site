Intelligence, not graphs

Everything under the hood

Powered by on-device statistical analysis, not black-box AI. Every insight comes with evidence and confidence levels.

○
Readiness Prediction
A weighted score from duration, efficiency, consistency, trends, and physiology. Know if today is a push day or a recovery day.
◎
Personal Baseline
14-day rolling median of your sleep metrics. Outlier-resistant. Every night compared to your norm, not someone else's.
★
Behavior Impact
Discover how caffeine, alcohol, exercise timing, stress, and screen time affect your sleep using Cohen's d effect size.
➤
Action Plans
Max 3 recommendations per day. Priority-ranked by impact. Time-aware — caffeine advice only appears before your cutoff.
◔
Recovery Windows
Optimal windows for peak alertness, deep focus, physical activity, and wind-down — adapted to your sleep schedule.
♥
HealthKit Native
Deep integration with Apple Health. Sleep stages, HRV, heart rate, respiratory rate, blood oxygen, and wrist temperature.
