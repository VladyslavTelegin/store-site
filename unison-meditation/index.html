FAQ

Common questions.

How does group sync work without the internet?
Unison Meditation uses peer-to-peer communication over your local Wi-Fi network. Devices discover each other automatically using Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework. No router internet access needed.
Can I use Unison Meditation completely offline?
Yes. Solo meditation works in airplane mode with no connectivity at all. Group sessions require local Wi-Fi only — no internet connection is used or needed.
Which devices are compatible?
Unison Meditation requires iPhone with iOS 17 or later. All participants in a group session need to be on the same local Wi-Fi network.
How many people can join a group session?
Group sessions support up to 8 participants on the same network. Each person sees a synchronized timer and hears bells at the same moment.
Is my meditation data shared with anyone?
Never. All session data is stored locally on your device. We don't operate servers and have no ability to access, collect, or view any of your data.
Can I cancel Premium anytime?
Yes. Premium is managed through your Apple ID subscriptions. Cancel anytime, and you keep access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
