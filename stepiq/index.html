The Decision

Three states. Zero guilt.

No goals, no rings, no streaks. stepiq evaluates your day and gives you one clear answer.

☑
Enough
"You've walked enough today"
You've met or exceeded your personal baseline. Rest guilt-free. You have permission to stop.
∼
Optional
"Walking more could help"
More walking could be beneficial, but isn't necessary. Completely your choice. Neither good nor bad.
☉
Beneficial
"A walk would help today"
A short walk would be beneficial based on your patterns. A gentle suggestion, never a demand.
