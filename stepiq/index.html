Common questions

Fair questions, honest answers.

"My phone already counts steps."

Yes, but does it tell you if that's enough? Your phone shows a number. stepiq shows a decision: you've walked enough, or you haven't. That's the difference between data and an answer.

"What goal should I set?"

Start with 8,000 steps. That's what most research points to for general health. Walk for a week, see how it feels, and adjust. There's no magic number -- just the one that works for your life.

"Is there a free version?"

Yes. Free gives you full daily step tracking with a basic goal. Pro adds weekly trends, home screen widgets, and custom goals. The free tier is genuinely useful, not a teaser.
