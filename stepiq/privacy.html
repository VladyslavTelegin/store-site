Privacy Policy

stepiq was built with a zero-server architecture. Your activity data never leaves your device. This policy explains exactly what we access, why, and what we don't do.

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 · Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

stepiq processes everything on your device. We operate zero servers. We have zero access to your data. Privacy is not a feature we added — it is the architecture we chose.

Your step count, workouts, and sleep data reveal intimate details about your daily life. That is why stepiq uses a strict on-device-only architecture. There is no backend, no analytics, and no way for us to see your data.

2. Data We Access

stepiq accesses the following data exclusively through Apple HealthKit, only with your explicit permission:

Data TypePurposeLeaves Device?
Step CountDaily walking assessment and baselineNo
Walking DistanceSupplementary activity metricNo
WorkoutsContext-aware walking needs adjustmentNo
Sleep AnalysisRecovery-aware assessmentsNo

All data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework. None of it is transmitted anywhere.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

stepiq does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • Any data from other apps

We have no analytics SDK, no tracking pixels, no server logs, and no user database. We literally cannot identify you.

4. Apple HealthKit

  • Read-only access. stepiq never writes data to HealthKit.
  • Explicit permission. You grant or deny access to each data type individually.
  • Revocable at any time in Settings > Health > Data Access > stepiq.
  • Not shared. HealthKit data is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.
  • Not transferred. All processing is on-device; data never leaves your phone.

5. How Data Is Processed

All processing happens locally: baseline calculations use a rolling 7-14 day median of your step data. The Decision Engine evaluates your current steps against your baseline, factoring in workouts, sleep, and time of day. All algorithms are deterministic and run on your device's processor.

There are no network calls for data processing. stepiq works fully offline after installation.

6. Data Storage

  • Encrypted at rest by iOS Data Protection
  • Sandboxed within the stepiq app container
  • Included in iCloud backups only if you have iCloud backup enabled (managed by iOS)
  • Deleted entirely when you uninstall stepiq

7. Third Parties

stepiq does not share data with any third parties. No analytics, no ads, no crash reporting, no social SDKs, no data brokers. The only external communication is Apple's StoreKit 2 for subscription verification.

8. Subscriptions & Payments

Optional Pro subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple. We have no access to your payment information or Apple ID.

9. Children's Privacy

stepiq is not directed at children under 13. Since we collect no personal information from any user, this concern is inherently mitigated.

10. Your Rights

  • Access: All data is on your device — you already have complete access.
  • Deletion: Uninstall stepiq to delete all app data.
  • Portability: Health data remains in Apple Health.
  • Restriction: Revoke specific permissions in Health settings.

11. Changes to This Policy

Our core commitment — your data stays on your device — will not change. Any architectural changes affecting this will require explicit in-app consent.
