stepiq uses a zero-server architecture. Your step data never leaves your device. This policy explains exactly what we access and what we don't.

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

stepiq stores everything on your device. We operate zero servers. We have zero access to your data. Privacy is not a feature — it is the architecture.

Your movement data is personal. How much you walk, when, and how often — that data belongs to you and only you.

2. Data We Access

stepiq stores the following data locally on your device:

Data TypePurposeLeaves Device?
Daily Step CountRead from HealthKit for daily trackingNo
Step GoalYour personal daily targetNo
Weekly HistoryTrend view of recent daysNo
Widget DataStep count for home screen widget (Pro)No
Theme PreferenceUI customizationNo

All data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

stepiq does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email, or personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • Any HealthKit data beyond step count
  • Any data from other apps

We have no analytics SDK, no tracking pixels, no server logs, and no user database. We literally cannot identify you.

4. HealthKit Usage

stepiq requests read-only access to your step count from Apple HealthKit. This data is read locally and never transmitted. stepiq does not write to HealthKit. You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings > Privacy > Health.

5. How Data Is Processed

All processing happens locally on your device. Goal comparison is a simple local calculation. There are no network calls for data processing. stepiq works fully offline after installation.

6. Data Storage

  • Encrypted at rest by iOS Data Protection
  • Sandboxed within the stepiq app container
  • Included in iCloud backups only if you have iCloud backup enabled
  • Deleted entirely when you uninstall stepiq

7. Third Parties

stepiq does not share data with any third parties. No analytics, no ads, no crash reporting. The only external communication is Apple's StoreKit 2 for subscription verification.

8. Subscriptions & Payments

Optional Pro subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple. We have no access to your payment information or Apple ID.

9. Children's Privacy

stepiq is not directed at children under 13. Since we collect no personal information, this concern is inherently mitigated.

10. Your Rights

  • Access: All data is on your device — you already have complete access.
  • Deletion: Uninstall stepiq to delete all app data.
  • HealthKit: Revoke access in Settings > Privacy > Health at any time.

11. Changes to This Policy

Our core commitment — your data stays on your device — will not change.
