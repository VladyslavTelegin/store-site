Your party, your rules

Pro tips

Become a better host

The best matchup hosts know how to keep the energy high and the guesses wild.

1

Start with easy rules

For first-time players, pick obvious categories like "Things you can eat." Let them feel the click of figuring it out before going harder.

2

Mix categories each round

Alternate between Form, Semantic, and Party rules to keep people guessing. Predictable hosts get cracked fast.

3

Use the timer strategically

Short timer (2 min) for easy rules creates panic. Long timer (5 min) for hard rules gives room to think. No timer for chill vibes.

4

Write inside-joke rules

Custom rules that reference your friend group are the funniest. "Things Dave would say" will have everyone in tears.

5

Let the debate happen

The best moments in matchup are the arguments. Is "ketchup" a drink? That debate IS the game. Lean into it.

6

Rotate hosts every round

Everyone should get a turn hosting. Different hosts bring different energy, and being the host is half the fun.
