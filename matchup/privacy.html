Privacy Policy

matchup works completely offline. We collect no data, require no accounts, and have no servers. This is the simplest privacy policy you'll ever read.

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 · Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. The Short Version

matchup collects zero data. We have zero servers. We require zero accounts. The app works entirely offline. There is nothing to disclose because there is nothing to collect.

2. How matchup Works

matchup is an offline local multiplayer party game. Players connect via Bluetooth or peer-to-peer WiFi using Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework. Alternatively, players can use Single Device mode (pass-and-play) which requires no connectivity at all.

All game data (rules, words, scores) exists only in memory during gameplay and is discarded when the game session ends. Custom saved rules (Pro feature) are stored locally on your device.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

To be absolutely clear, matchup does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • Game content or chat messages
  • Player names (chosen locally, never transmitted to us)

matchup does not connect to the internet at all. There are no network calls to any server, ever. We cannot collect data even if we wanted to.

4. Local Multiplayer Communication

When players connect via Bluetooth or peer-to-peer WiFi:

  • Direct device-to-device. Communication happens directly between phones using Apple's Multipeer Connectivity. No data passes through any server.
  • Temporary. All game data is in-memory only and is lost when the session ends.
  • No internet. Multipeer Connectivity works without any internet connection.

5. Data Storage

  • Game sessions are stored in memory only and discarded when the app closes
  • Custom saved rules (Pro) are stored locally on your device
  • All local data is deleted when you uninstall matchup
  • No cloud sync, no backups to our servers (we have none)

6. Third Parties

matchup does not include any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social features. The only external framework is Apple's StoreKit 2 for the optional one-time Pro purchase, handled entirely by Apple.

7. Purchases

matchup Pro is a one-time in-app purchase ($4.99) processed entirely by Apple. We have no access to your payment information or Apple ID.

8. Children's Privacy

matchup includes a Family Safe Mode that filters rules for all ages. Since we collect no data from any user, children's privacy is inherently protected.

9. Your Rights

Since matchup collects no data, there is no data to access, delete, or port. Uninstalling the app removes everything.
