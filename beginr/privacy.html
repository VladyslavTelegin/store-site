Privacy Policy

beginr was built with a zero-server architecture. Your session data never leaves your device. This policy explains exactly what we access, why, and what we don't do.

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

beginr stores everything on your device. We operate zero servers. We have zero access to your data. Privacy is not a feature we added — it is the architecture we chose.

Your productivity patterns are personal. When you started, how long you continued, what you were working on — that data belongs to you and only you. beginr uses a strict on-device-only architecture. There is no backend, no analytics, and no way for us to see your data.

2. Data We Access

beginr stores the following data locally on your device:

Data TypePurposeLeaves Device?
Session TimestampsRecord when sessions started and endedNo
Session CountDaily session tracking (free tier limit)No
Task NamesOptional labels you assign to sessionsNo
Theme PreferenceUI customization settingNo
Reminder SettingsNotification scheduling (Pro)No

All data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework. None of it is transmitted anywhere.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

beginr does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports
  • HealthKit or health data of any kind
  • Any data from other apps
  • Keyboard input or screen content

We have no analytics SDK, no tracking pixels, no server logs, and no user database. We literally cannot identify you.

4. How Data Is Processed

All processing happens locally on your device. Session timing uses the device clock. Daily session counting is a simple local counter that resets at midnight. There are no algorithms analyzing your behavior, no recommendations engine, and no data sent externally.

There are no network calls for data processing. beginr works fully offline after installation.

5. Data Storage

  • Encrypted at rest by iOS Data Protection
  • Sandboxed within the beginr app container
  • Included in iCloud backups only if you have iCloud backup enabled (managed by iOS)
  • Deleted entirely when you uninstall beginr

6. Third Parties

beginr does not share data with any third parties. No analytics, no ads, no crash reporting, no social SDKs, no data brokers. The only external communication is Apple's StoreKit 2 for subscription verification.

7. Subscriptions & Payments

Optional Pro subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple. We have no access to your payment information or Apple ID. Subscription status is verified locally through StoreKit 2.

8. Children's Privacy

beginr is not directed at children under 13. Since we collect no personal information from any user, this concern is inherently mitigated.

9. Your Rights

  • Access: All data is on your device — you already have complete access.
  • Deletion: Uninstall beginr to delete all app data.
  • Restriction: Revoke notification permissions in Settings at any time.
  • Objection: Since we collect nothing, there is nothing to object to.

10. Changes to This Policy

Our core commitment — your data stays on your device — will not change. Any architectural changes affecting this will require explicit in-app consent.
