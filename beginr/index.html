Philosophy

Procrastination is not a character flaw. It's a starting problem.

beginr removes every barrier between you and starting. No configuration. No goals. No tracking. Just a 5-minute timer and a single question: do you want to continue?

▶
Starting is the achievement
Not finishing. Not optimizing. Just beginning. That's the only metric that matters.
■
Stopping is celebrated
After 5 minutes, STOP is the primary action. There is no guilt. You already won.
✕
No streaks. No gamification.
No leaderboards, no badges, no shame. Your relationship with productivity is yours alone.
🔒
Zero-server architecture
Your data stays on your device. We can't see it. We don't want to.
