What Is CLUXTER?

Think of it as an orchestra of light — each device is an instrument, each scene is a composition, and the cluster is the ensemble.

CLUXTER is a distributed light orchestration system for iOS and iPadOS. Place multiple iPhones and iPads in a room, and they self-organize into a synchronized cluster — each device assuming a unique role (Beacon, Pulse, Echo, Accent, Shadow). Using screen color, torch intensity, and precise timing, the cluster performs coordinated light patterns that no single device could achieve alone. No server. No internet. No wires. Just light, perfectly synchronized.

Features

Every feature, no asterisks

A complete breakdown of every capability in CLUXTER. Not "and more" — this is all of it.

🔗 Cluster & Sync

The distributed engine that makes multi-device orchestration possible.

🔗

Adaptive Cluster Strategy

Devices self-organize with automatic topology discovery. No manual pairing — just open the app and the cluster forms itself, choosing the optimal structure for the number and positions of devices.

🎭

Role-Based Nodes

Each device is assigned a distinct role: Beacon (leader), Pulse (rhythm), Echo (response), Accent (highlight), or Shadow (ambient). Roles determine behavior, timing, and light character.

🔄

Dynamic Role Morphing

Roles are not static. During scene execution, devices can shift roles — a Pulse node becomes an Accent, an Echo transforms into a Shadow — creating evolving, organic light compositions.

⏱

Scheduled Sync

NTP-inspired timestamp-based clock synchronization. Every node aligns to a shared clock with sub-millisecond precision, ensuring light events fire simultaneously across the entire cluster.

🌊

Temporal Elasticity Pro

Network latency is not a bug — it is a creative tool. Intentional delay between nodes creates natural wave and ripple effects that feel organic rather than mechanical.

📈

Degradation by Design

As the cluster grows beyond optimal size, quality reduces gracefully — simpler patterns, wider timing tolerances — so the experience never breaks, it just adapts.

💓

Sync Health Score

A real-time 0–100 score visible on every device. Monitor cluster synchronization quality at a glance. Know instantly if a node is drifting or the network is straining.

🛡

Fail-Soft Nodes

If a device disconnects, it does not go dark. It continues playing the last known pattern autonomously until it reconnects, ensuring the visual experience never abruptly breaks.

🎧

Leader Participation

The Beacon node does not just coordinate — it plays. Unlike traditional conductor architectures, the leader is a full participant in the light performance.

🕴

Silent Conductor Mode Pro

For advanced setups: the conductor device controls the cluster without emitting any light itself. Pure orchestration from behind the scenes, invisible to the audience.

🖐 Interaction & Experience

How humans and devices communicate through light, gesture, and presence.

🤝

Light Handshake

Two devices trade blink patterns to confirm identity. A visible, physical handshake made of light — no abstract QR codes or pairing screens needed.

🗳

Silent Voting

Nodes vote via brightness level. Want the cluster to decide something? Each device expresses its vote through light intensity — a democratic protocol built into the visual language.

👁

Eyes-Closed Mode

Torch-only, screen off, no visual UI. Place devices face-down and they communicate purely through their torch LEDs. For environments where screen glow would be distracting.

✨

Zero-UI Mode

The app disappears entirely. No buttons, no controls, no interface — only light remains. The ultimate performance mode where technology becomes invisible.

🖐

Gesture-as-Signal

Shake or tap a device to trigger cluster-wide events. A physical shake becomes a broadcast signal that ripples through the entire cluster as a coordinated light response.

📡

Proximity Intelligence Pro

Spatial awareness enables wave effects. Devices understand their relative positions and use that knowledge to create light that moves through physical space.

🚶

Human Proximity Waves Pro

Light follows human movement. Using device sensors, the cluster detects when someone moves near a device and triggers cascading light responses that track the person.

💓

Cluster Heartbeat

A periodic sync pulse visible across the entire cluster. Like a shared heartbeat, every device flashes in unison at regular intervals to maintain cohesion and confirm liveness.

⚡

Cluster Tension

Nodes can express disagreement through light. When sync drifts or roles conflict, devices show visible tension through flickering or color shifts — making the system state tangible.

🟠

Collective Stillness

All nodes hold absolute stillness together. A coordinated moment of perfect, unwavering light — no flicker, no drift — that requires more precision than any animation.

🌑

Shared Silence Mode

Coordinated darkness as expression. Every device goes dark simultaneously, creating a moment of collective silence. The absence of light becomes as powerful as its presence.

🔒 Safety & Trust

Protection, verification, and emergency systems built into every layer.

🔒

Verified Cluster

A shared secret handshake ensures only authorized devices can join. Visible light confirmation makes spoofing impossible.

📈

Trust Gradient

Trust levels build over session history. New devices start with limited capabilities and earn full access through consistent, verified participation.

🚫

Cluster Lockdown

Prevent unauthorized joins at any time. Lock the cluster with a single tap and no new devices can enter until you explicitly unlock.

⚠

Emergency Authority Override

Any single node can trigger a safety lockdown. One tap on any device immediately brings the entire cluster to a safe state. No hierarchy required.

🚨

Directional SOS Gradient

An SOS pattern with spatial direction. The cluster creates a light gradient that points toward the nearest exit, using device positions for directional guidance.

🟦

Safe Corridor

Emergency exit lighting. Devices along a path illuminate sequentially to create a visible safe corridor guiding people toward exits during emergencies.

🛡

Guardian Nodes

Designate specific devices as safety monitors. Guardian nodes watch for dangerous patterns, thermal spikes, and photosensitivity risks across the entire cluster.

💡

Photosensitivity Protection

Flash rate is hard-capped at 3Hz across the entire system. No scene, no bug, no edge case can ever exceed the medically safe flash frequency.

🌡

Thermal Awareness

Every device monitors its own temperature. If a device gets too warm, CLUXTER automatically reduces torch intensity and screen brightness to prevent overheating.

Cluster Aging Pro

Behavior evolves over time. A cluster that has performed together many times develops subtle variations — smoother transitions, tighter sync — like a band that has played together for years.

Light Scarcity Mode Pro

💰

Light Economy Pro

Light as a shared resource. Nodes can trade, pool, and redistribute light budget across the cluster, creating a micro-economy of visual expression.

🧬

Cluster Fingerprint

Every cluster combination produces a unique light signature. The same scene looks subtly different on every cluster, making each performance genuinely one-of-a-kind.

🔄

No-Repeat Guarantee

The pattern engine tracks history. CLUXTER ensures that within a session, the same exact pattern never repeats, keeping every moment fresh and unpredictable.

🌊

Latency-as-Effect Pro

Intentional delay between nodes creates natural wave and cascade effects. What other systems would hide as a flaw, CLUXTER uses as a creative tool.

💔

Failure Ceremony

When a device disconnects, it does not just vanish. The cluster performs a graceful disconnect ritual — a visual farewell that acknowledges the departing node.

💯

Cluster Signature Reveal

The cluster can reveal its unique identity through a synchronized display. A visual fingerprint that proves this specific combination of devices performed together.

🌐

Social Physics Pro

Node behavior is influenced by neighbors. Nearby devices subtly affect each other's patterns, creating emergent behavior that no single node was programmed to produce.

The cluster slows together over time. As a session extends, the light becomes calmer, softer, more contemplative — mimicking the natural energy arc of a live performance.

A ghost of the previous light state persists briefly. When a pattern changes, the old state fades slowly rather than cutting instantly, creating visual depth and continuity.
