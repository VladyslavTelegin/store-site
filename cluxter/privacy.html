1. Our Privacy Promise

CLUXTER operates on a zero-server architecture. We run no servers, collect no data, and have no access to your information. Every piece of data stays on your device. Privacy is not a feature — it is the foundation of how CLUXTER is built.

CLUXTER is a distributed light orchestration system for iOS and iPadOS. It coordinates flashlights across nearby devices using peer-to-peer networking that never touches the internet. We believe that controlling lights in a room should not require surrendering your privacy. That is why we built CLUXTER with no backend, no accounts, no analytics, and no way for us to ever see what you do with the app.

2. Data We Access

CLUXTER accesses the following data categories on your device, only with your explicit permission where required by iOS:

Data Type Purpose Leaves Device?
Camera (Torch Only) Flashlight/torch control — no images are captured or stored No
Local Network Device discovery and cluster synchronization via MultipeerConnectivity No
UserDefaults Trial date tracking and user preferences No
StoreKit In-app purchase verification (handled by Apple) No

All data accessed by CLUXTER is processed and stored entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to any external server.

Camera Access

CLUXTER requests camera permission solely to control the device's flashlight (torch). The camera sensor itself is used only to activate and modulate the LED torch. No images or video are ever captured, processed, or stored. The camera viewfinder is never activated. If you deny camera access, torch functionality will be unavailable, but all other features will continue to work.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

To be absolutely clear, CLUXTER does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your contacts or address book
  • Your photos, videos, or media library
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Usage analytics or behavioral telemetry
  • Advertising identifiers or device fingerprints
  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Crash reports (we do not use any crash reporting SDK)

We have no analytics SDK, no tracking pixels, no server logs, and no user database. We literally cannot identify you.

4. Peer-to-Peer Communication

CLUXTER uses Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework to enable device-to-device communication. This is how clusters of devices coordinate light patterns together.

How It Works

MultipeerConnectivity establishes direct connections between nearby Apple devices over local Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. All communication happens device-to-device on your local network. No data is routed through the internet or any external server.

What Data Is Exchanged

When devices form a cluster, the following data is exchanged locally between connected peers:

  • Sync timestamps — used to coordinate timing across devices
  • Light commands — torch on/off, brightness levels, and pattern instructions
  • Role assignments — which device is the leader and which are followers in the cluster

Encryption

All peer-to-peer communication is encrypted by the MultipeerConnectivity framework itself. CLUXTER does not implement its own encryption layer because Apple's framework provides transport-level encryption by default. No cluster data ever leaves the local network.

5. Scene & Preference Storage

CLUXTER stores your light scenes and app preferences locally on your device:

  • Scenes are stored in a local JSON format within the app's sandboxed container
  • User preferences (such as default brightness, preferred cluster roles, and UI settings) are stored in UserDefaults
  • Trial date is stored in UserDefaults to manage the trial period

All of this data is:

  • Encrypted at rest by iOS Data Protection (tied to your device passcode)
  • Sandboxed within the CLUXTER app container — inaccessible to other apps
  • Fully deletable by uninstalling CLUXTER from your device
  • Never transmitted to any server or third party

6. In-App Purchases

CLUXTER offers optional in-app purchases through the Apple App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card number, or Apple ID.

  • Payments are processed by Apple through your App Store account
  • Purchase receipts are verified locally on-device using Apple's StoreKit framework
  • No purchase data is transmitted to our servers (we have no servers)
  • Manage your purchases through Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions

7. Children's Privacy

CLUXTER does not collect personal information from any user, regardless of age. Since our zero-server architecture means no data is ever collected or transmitted, CLUXTER is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by design. There are no accounts, no registrations, and no personal data collection of any kind.

8. Third-Party Services

CLUXTER does not integrate with any third-party services. Specifically:

  • No analytics providers (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar)
  • No advertising networks (no ads of any kind)
  • No crash reporting services (no Firebase Crashlytics, Sentry, or similar)
  • No social integrations (no Facebook SDK, no social login)
  • No third-party SDKs of any kind
  • No data brokers

The only external service CLUXTER communicates with is the Apple App Store for purchase verification, which is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

CLUXTER retains data only on your device for as long as the app is installed:

  • Uninstall the app and all data is permanently deleted — scenes, preferences, trial information, and everything else
  • There is no cloud backup managed by CLUXTER (standard iOS/iCloud backups are managed by Apple at the OS level)
  • There is no "account" to delete because no account exists
  • There are no servers to request data deletion from because we operate no servers

Delete the app = delete all data. It is that simple. There is nothing left behind on any server because there are no servers.

10. Your Rights

Because CLUXTER operates with a zero-server, on-device-only architecture:

  • Access: All your data is on your device. You already have complete access to it.
  • Deletion: Uninstall CLUXTER to delete all app data instantly and permanently.
  • Portability: Data portability requests are not applicable because all data exists solely on your device — it is already in your possession.
  • Restriction: You can revoke camera and local network permissions at any time through iOS Settings.
  • Objection: You can stop all data processing by simply closing or uninstalling the app.

For users in the European Union, California, or other jurisdictions with specific privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.): CLUXTER's architecture means we are not a data controller or processor of your personal data in any networked sense. All data remains under your exclusive control on your device.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date above. Since CLUXTER does not collect email addresses, we cannot notify you via email. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Our core commitment — that your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party — will not change. If we ever change our architecture in a way that affects this commitment, we will require your explicit consent through an in-app prompt before any data leaves your device.
