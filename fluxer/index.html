FAQ

Questions & answers

Everything you need to know about Fluxer.

What file types can I transfer?

Any file your device can store — documents, photos, videos, folders, archives, app exports, and more. There are no file type restrictions.

Does it work between iPhone and Mac?

Fluxer works between any two iOS devices. Mac support and cross-platform transfers are on our roadmap for a future update.

How fast are transfers?

Fluxer uses Wi-Fi Direct for transfers, achieving speeds up to 50 MB/s. Actual speed depends on your devices and distance between them.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. Fluxer works entirely over local connections (Bluetooth for discovery, Wi-Fi Direct for transfer). No internet required.

Does the Apple Watch app work standalone?

The Apple Watch app monitors and controls transfers initiated from your iPhone. It shows real-time progress, speed, and lets you start saved quick-send presets.

What happens if my connection drops?

Fluxer automatically saves your transfer progress. When devices reconnect, the transfer resumes from where it stopped — no data is re-sent.
