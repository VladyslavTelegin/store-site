Privacy Policy

Fluxer transfers files directly between devices. Your files never touch our servers — because we don't have any. This policy explains exactly what we access, why, and what we don't do.

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

Fluxer is built on peer-to-peer technology. Files travel directly from one device to another. We operate zero servers, store zero files, and have zero access to your data. Privacy is not a feature — it is the architecture.

We believe your files are your business. Photos, documents, videos — none of it should pass through a third-party server just to reach the device sitting next to you. That is why we built Fluxer with a strict device-to-device architecture. There is no cloud relay. There is no temporary storage. There is no way for us to intercept, view, or log your transfers.

2. Data We Access

Fluxer accesses the following data categories, only with your explicit permission:

Data TypePurposeLeaves Device?
Selected FilesTransfer to the receiving devicePeer only
Device NameIdentify your device to nearby peersLocal only
Transfer HistoryDisplay recent transfers in-appNo

Files are sent directly to the receiving device using Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework or local Wi-Fi. They never pass through any server.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

To be absolutely clear, Fluxer does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprints
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral telemetry
  • Crash reports (we do not use any crash reporting SDK)
  • File contents, names, or metadata sent to our servers
  • Any data from other apps on your device

We have no analytics SDK, no tracking pixels, no server logs, and no user database. We literally cannot identify you.

4. Bluetooth & Local Network

Fluxer uses Bluetooth and local Wi-Fi to discover and connect with nearby devices:

  • Bluetooth. Used to discover nearby devices running Fluxer. Bluetooth is only active during device discovery and transfers.
  • Local Network. Used for high-speed file transfers over Wi-Fi Direct. No internet connection is required.
  • Explicit permission. iOS will prompt you to allow Bluetooth and Local Network access. You can revoke these permissions at any time in Settings.
  • No internet. Fluxer works in airplane mode. Transfers happen over direct device-to-device connections, not the internet.

5. How Data Is Processed

All data processing in Fluxer happens locally between the sending and receiving devices:

File Transfer

When you select files to send, Fluxer compresses and streams them directly to the receiving device using encrypted peer-to-peer connections. No intermediate server is involved.

Device Discovery

Fluxer broadcasts your device name over Bluetooth Low Energy to nearby devices. This name is only visible to other Fluxer users in proximity and is not transmitted over the internet.

There are no network calls to any server. Fluxer works fully offline after installation.

6. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device:

  • Transfer history is stored locally and shows recent files sent and received
  • Encrypted at rest by iOS Data Protection (tied to your device passcode)
  • Sandboxed within the Fluxer app container — inaccessible to other apps
  • Deleted entirely when you uninstall Fluxer from your device

We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud storage. There is no "account" to delete because no account exists.

7. Third Parties

Fluxer does not share data with any third parties. Specifically:

  • No analytics providers (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar)
  • No advertising networks (no ads of any kind)
  • No crash reporting services (no Firebase Crashlytics, Sentry, or similar)
  • No social integrations (no Facebook SDK, no social login)
  • No data brokers

The only external service Fluxer communicates with is the Apple App Store for purchase verification, which is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit 2 framework and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

8. Payments

Fluxer offers a 3-day free trial followed by a one-time purchase of $5.99, processed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card number, or Apple ID.

  • Payments are processed by Apple through your App Store account
  • Purchase status is verified on-device using Apple's StoreKit 2
  • No purchase data is transmitted to our servers (we have no servers)

9. Children's Privacy

Fluxer is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since Fluxer collects no personal information from any user, this concern is inherently mitigated by our architecture.

10. Your Rights

Because Fluxer operates with a zero-server, on-device-only architecture:

  • Access: All your data is on your device. You already have complete access.
  • Deletion: Uninstall Fluxer to delete all app data.
  • Portability: Transferred files remain in your device's file system.
  • Restriction: You can revoke Bluetooth and Local Network permissions at any time in Settings.
  • Objection: You can stop all processing by simply closing or uninstalling the app.

For users in the European Union, California, or other jurisdictions with specific privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.): Fluxer's architecture means we are not a data controller or processor of your personal data in any networked sense. All data remains under your control on your device.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date above. Our core commitment — that your files travel directly between devices and never through our servers — will not change.
