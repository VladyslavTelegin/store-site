Privacy Policy

RoomRaid uses your camera for AR gameplay — but everything stays on your device. We process no images on servers, require no accounts, and collect no data. This policy explains exactly how it works.

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

RoomRaid processes your camera feed entirely on your device using Apple's ARKit. No images, video, or room data are ever transmitted to any server. We have zero servers, zero access to your data, and zero interest in changing that.

We understand that an app requesting camera access carries a higher responsibility for privacy. Your camera sees your home, your friends, your personal space. That's why we built RoomRaid with an absolute on-device-only policy. ARKit processes the camera feed in real-time to place virtual critters in your room — and that data never leaves your device.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

To be absolutely clear, RoomRaid does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprints
  • Location data
  • Photos or recordings from your camera
  • Room layout data or 3D maps of your space
  • Usage analytics or behavioral telemetry
  • Crash reports (we do not use any crash reporting SDK)
  • Audio recordings from your microphone

RoomRaid does not connect to the internet for any game functionality. There are no network calls to any server. We cannot see your camera feed, your room, or anything you do in the app.

4. Camera, Local Network & Microphone

Camera

RoomRaid requires camera access to deliver the AR experience:

  • Real-time processing only. The camera feed is processed frame-by-frame by ARKit to detect surfaces and track motion. No frames are saved, stored, or transmitted.
  • No photos or recordings. RoomRaid does not take screenshots, record video, or capture any images from your camera.
  • Processed by ARKit. All AR processing uses Apple's on-device ARKit framework. The camera data never leaves the ARKit pipeline on your device.
  • Revocable. You can revoke camera access at any time in Settings > RoomRaid > Camera.

Local Network

For multiplayer games, RoomRaid uses the local network to connect players:

  • Peer-to-peer. Players connect directly using Apple's Multipeer Connectivity. No game data passes through any server.
  • No internet required. Multiplayer works in airplane mode over local Wi-Fi or Bluetooth.
  • Optional. Solo mode works without any network permissions.

Microphone (Optional)

Some game modes feature sound-reactive gameplay:

  • Real-time only. Audio is analyzed in real-time for game mechanics (e.g., detecting loudness to scare critters). No audio is recorded or stored.
  • Completely optional. You can decline microphone access and still play all core game modes.
  • No recordings. RoomRaid never records, saves, or transmits any audio.

5. How Data Is Processed

All data processing happens locally on your device:

AR Scene Processing

ARKit analyzes your camera feed to understand room geometry, detect surfaces, and track device motion. Virtual critters are placed onto detected surfaces. All of this processing happens entirely on your device's GPU and Neural Engine.

Game Logic

Scoring, critter behavior, and game state are computed locally. In multiplayer, minimal sync data (scores, critter positions) is shared peer-to-peer between devices.

There are no server calls for any game feature. RoomRaid works fully offline after installation.

6. Data Storage

  • Game sessions are stored in memory during gameplay and discarded when the session ends
  • High scores are stored locally on your device
  • No camera data is ever persisted to storage
  • Encrypted at rest by iOS Data Protection
  • Deleted entirely when you uninstall RoomRaid

We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud storage. There is no "account" to delete because no account exists.

7. Third Parties

RoomRaid does not share data with any third parties. Specifically:

  • No analytics providers
  • No advertising networks (no ads of any kind)
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social integrations
  • No data brokers

The only external service is the Apple App Store for purchase verification via StoreKit 2, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

8. Payments

RoomRaid offers a free tier and a one-time Full Game purchase ($6.99) processed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card number, or Apple ID.

  • Payments are processed by Apple through your App Store account
  • Purchase status is verified on-device using Apple's StoreKit 2
  • No purchase data is transmitted to our servers (we have no servers)

9. Children's Privacy

RoomRaid is family-friendly. Since we collect no personal information from any user, children's privacy is inherently protected by our zero-data architecture. Parental supervision is recommended for AR gameplay to ensure physical safety.

10. Your Rights

Because RoomRaid operates with a zero-server, on-device-only architecture:

  • Access: All your data is on your device. You already have complete access.
  • Deletion: Uninstall RoomRaid to delete all app data.
  • Restriction: Revoke Camera, Local Network, or Microphone permissions at any time in Settings.
  • Objection: You can stop all processing by simply closing or uninstalling the app.

For users in the European Union, California, or other jurisdictions with specific privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.): RoomRaid's architecture means we are not a data controller or processor of your personal data in any networked sense.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last Updated" date above. Our core commitment — that your camera feed and personal data never leave your device — will not change.
