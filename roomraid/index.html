FAQ

Got questions?

RoomRaid requires an iPhone or iPad with an A12 chip or later running iOS 17+. This includes iPhone XS and newer, iPad Pro (3rd gen+), iPad Air (3rd gen+), and iPad mini (5th gen+). The device must have a LiDAR sensor or TrueDepth camera for the best AR experience.
RoomRaid uses Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework, which creates a direct device-to-device connection via Bluetooth and Wi-Fi Direct. No router or internet connection needed -- just have all players in the same room with Bluetooth and Wi-Fi turned on.
Absolutely. Your camera feed is processed entirely on-device using Apple's ARKit. No images, video frames, or spatial data are ever sent anywhere. We don't have servers, cloud storage, or any way to access your camera data. Everything stays on your iPhone.
Any room works! RoomRaid automatically adapts to your space. A living room, bedroom, or even a dorm room is perfect. Larger spaces mean critters spread out more, while smaller rooms create intense, fast-paced hunts. We recommend at least 8x8 feet for the best experience.
Yes! RoomRaid is rated 4+ on the App Store. All critters are cute and friendly. There's no violence, no inappropriate content, no ads, and no social features that connect to strangers. It's designed for families and friends of all ages.
Absolutely. The free version includes Solo Hunt mode with 3 critter types and full AR features. You can play unlimited rounds, track your high scores, and get a complete feel for the game. The full unlock adds multiplayer, all 6 critter types, sound-reactive features, and Team Battle mode.
